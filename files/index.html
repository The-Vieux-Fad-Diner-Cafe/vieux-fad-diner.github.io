What is a Diner ?

A diner is feeling good, being happy and enjoying being in company and savoring a maxi hamburger or a chocolate milkshake to the sound of Rock ‘n’ Roll. Created in the early 1950s in the United States as snack bars located along American highways and in response to the period of great sadness caused by the Second World War, diners are characterized by their unique and original furnishing style: checkered flooring, rounded lines, neon lighting and special tables at which to enjoy a menu crammed with loads of goodies - all made in the USA.
